    15.
    Find the least common denominator (LCD) of the rational expressions:
    \begin{equation*} \frac{6t+1}{9t^{2}+12t+4} \mbox{ and } \frac{2t+5}{9t^{2}-4} \end{equation*}
    LCD:
    Answer.
    \(\left(3t-2\right)\!\left(3t+2\right)^{2}\)
    Solution.
    The factors of the denominators are
    \(9t^{2}+12t+4 = \left(3t+2\right)^{2}\)
    \(9t^{2}-4= \left(3t+2\right)\!\left(3t-2\right)\)
    Hence, the LCD is \(\left(3t-2\right)\!\left(3t+2\right)^{2}\text{.}\)
