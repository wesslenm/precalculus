Common Errors.

The following expressions are not equal, in general.
\begin{equation} (x+y)^2 \not= x^2 + y^2\tag{2.1.8} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} (x+y)^a \not= x^a + y^a\tag{2.1.9} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} \sqrt{x+y} \not= \sqrt{x} + \sqrt{y}\tag{2.1.10} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} x^{a}y^{b} \not= (xy)^{ab}\tag{2.1.11} \end{equation}
\begin{equation} \frac{x^a}{y^b} \not= \left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^{a-b} \tag{2.1.12} \end{equation}
