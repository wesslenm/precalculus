Definitions of trigonometric functions - angles in standard position.

Let \(\theta \) be any angle in standard position in a circle centered at \((0,0)\text{.}\)
  • \(\displaystyle \sin \theta = \frac{y}{r}\)
  • \(\displaystyle \cos \theta = \frac{x}{r}\)
  • \(\displaystyle \tan \theta = \frac{y}{x}\)
